==About== A good tactile pushbutton. Useful when something larger than the standard 6mm version is desired. These also have better pin spacing for using in a breadboard. ==Common Uses== This part is commonly used as: *a reset button for a microcontroller. *a button on a keypad. *a larger button to distinguish it from more standard pushbuttons. ==Specifications==
